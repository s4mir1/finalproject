About HabitFlow

HabitFlow is a simple yet powerful habit tracking application designed to help you build consistent positive habits and break negative ones. Research shows that it takes an average of 66 days to form a new habit, and our app is here to support you throughout that journey.

Key Features

  • Simple Habit Tracking: Easily add habits and mark them as complete each day
  • Streak Tracking: Monitor your consistency with visual streak counters
  • Progress Visualization: See your progress with intuitive progress bars
  • Category Organization: Organize habits by categories like Health, Fitness, Learning
  • Analytics Dashboard: Get insights into your habit-building journey
  • Responsive Design: Works perfectly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices

How to Use

  1. Go to the Habit Tracker page
  2. Enter a habit name and select a category
  3. Click "Add Habit" to create your new habit
  4. Each day, click "Mark Complete" when you finish the habit
  5. Watch your streak grow and stay motivated!
  6. Check the Analytics page to see your overall progress
